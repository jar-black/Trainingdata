The Day of Judgment

When the day of judgment comes,
when heaven opens
and the angels descend,
when the dead arise from their graves
and all gather for the judgmentâ€”

What shall then the judgment be?
Who shall then be acquitted
and who shall be condemned?

Tell me: He who loved much,
shall he be condemned for his sins,
or shall he be acquitted for his love?

Tell me: He who suffered much,
shall he get reward for his torments,
or shall he be punished for his complaining?

I know not what the judgment will be.
But one thing know I:
that love is greater than judgment,
and that mercy
is greater than justice.
